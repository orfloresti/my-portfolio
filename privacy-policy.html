1. Introduction

Welcome to MyPortfolio (the "App"), a personal portfolio application developed by Orlando Flores Teomitzi ("Developer," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

The App does not require user accounts or login credentials. We do not collect personal information through any forms or registration processes.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, device identifier
  • Usage Statistics: Pages visited, features accessed, time spent in the App
  • Crash Reports: Diagnostic data to help us improve App stability (if enabled by platform)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to understand how users interact with the App

2.3 Third-Party Services

The App utilizes the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Fonts: Used for typography – Collects minimal device metadata
    • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • URL Launcher: Used to open external links (social media, websites) – Does not collect data directly
  • Font Awesome: Icon library used in the App
    • Visit: https://fontawesome.com/

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve functionality and user experience
  • Performance: Maintain and optimize App performance
  • Troubleshooting: Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Analytics: Understand user engagement and features usage
  • Compliance: Ensure compliance with legal obligations and platform policies

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most data collected is stored locally on your device. The App itself does not maintain centralized servers for user data storage.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for any data transmission
  • Regular security assessments
  • Compliance with platform security standards (iOS, Android, Web)

4.3 Data Retention

Automatically collected information is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Crash reports and analytics data are typically retained for 90 days.

5. Information Sharing

5.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share technical data with third-party service providers that help us operate the App:

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to:

6. User Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Understand what data is collected
  • Request information about your data
  • Delete data stored locally on your device by uninstalling the App

6.2 Opt-Out Options

You can control your data collection:

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of collected personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: No discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitations on how your data is processed
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

Our legal basis for processing data is legitimate interests and compliance with applicable laws. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.
